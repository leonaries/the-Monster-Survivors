Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling roguelike adventure!

Game Features

  • Endless Gameplay
  • Multiple Character Classes
  • Unique Power-ups

How to Play

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to Move
  • Collect Experience to Level Up
  • Choose Upgrades Wisely

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting roguelike survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, level up your abilities, and see how long you can survive in this challenging adventure. With each run being unique, you'll discover new strategies and combinations to overcome the increasing difficulty.
